# Anti-Patterns (Content Module)

Front-load clarity. Cut hype. Keep structure disciplined.

## Banned Openings

- "In today's fast-paced world..."
- "We are excited to announce..."
- "Let's dive in."
- "Game changer:" (as a standalone opener)
- "This changes everything."
- "You won't believe..."
- "Hot take:" (unless a real argument follows immediately)

## Banned Words/Phrases

### Tier 1 (Never Use)

- leverage
- synergy
- disruptive
- revolutionary (unless historically precise)
- paradigm shift
- world-class
- cutting-edge
- unlock your potential
- next-level
- best-in-class

### Tier 2 (Use Only If Literally True)

- guaranteed
- proven
- always
- never
- everyone
- no one
- impossible
- obvious
- effortless
- instant

### Tier 3 (Prefer Concrete Alternatives)

- amazing
- incredible
- super
- huge
- massive
- very
- really
- basically
- simply
- clearly
- obviously
- literally
- seamless
- robust
- impactful

## Structural Traps

- Forced rule-of-three when two points are enough
- Excessive hedging ("maybe", "sort of", "kind of")
- Generic press-release tone without evidence
- Abstract claims without examples or numbers
- CTA that does not match the content's actual claim
- Repeating the same point across hook, claim, and takeaway

## Constraint

- Max 1 em-dash per paragraph.

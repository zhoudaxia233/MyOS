# Piaget Boundary Learning Notes (2026-03-12)

## Source

- Type: course takeaway
- Context: condensed notes from a Jordan Peterson section on Piaget
- Ingestion intent: audited absorption for MyOS learning membrane (candidate -> review -> promote/reject)

## Core Material

1. Abstract personality traits are built bottom-up from behavior.
- Trait labels (for example, "good" or "resilient") are abstractions over repeated micro-actions.
- Identity change should begin with controllable micro-routines instead of abstract relabeling.

2. Growth is strongest at the boundary between order and chaos.
- Full stasis blocks growth.
- Too much unknown at once causes breakdown.
- Sustainable growth comes from incremental restructuring with bounded novelty load.

3. Emotional regulation should be pushed down to concrete behavior.
- Under failure/conflict, thinking tends toward total self-negation or total other-negation.
- Corrective move: de-escalate to the smallest controllable behavior rather than staying in abstract collapse.

## MyOS Constraint Reminder

- Do not overwrite judgment core directly.
- Keep abstractions non-authoritative until candidate review and promotion.

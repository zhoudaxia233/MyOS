# Reverse Thinking Stories (2026-03-05)

## Source

- Type: video summary
- Topic: reverse thinking in 3 layers

## Core Extraction

1. Exit thinking:
   - When option A/B are both blocked, search for ignored option C.
   - Reframe constraints instead of repeating failed attempts.
2. Game thinking:
   - Reduce direct confrontation.
   - Design incentives around loss aversion, self-image, and intrinsic motivation.
3. Essence thinking:
   - Identify hidden mechanism (scarcity, positioning, rule design, incentive chain).
   - Distinguish real customer value from being consumed by the business loop.

## Transferable Checks

- Before execution, ask: "What is the side-door option C here?"
- Before negotiation, ask: "What human motive can align cooperation?"
- Before committing resources, ask: "Where is the true value chain and who captures it?"
